# 🏪 Credit Management & Store Sales System

A modern and scalable platform for managing store operations, customer accounts, credit sales, payments, inventory, and financial records.

---

## 📖 Overview

Many small and medium-sized businesses still rely on notebooks, spreadsheets, or manual processes to track customer debts and sales. As the number of customers grows, managing outstanding balances, payment histories, and inventory becomes increasingly difficult and error-prone.

This project aims to provide a reliable digital solution that simplifies daily store operations while improving accuracy, transparency, and efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Simplify customer debt management
* Track credit and cash sales accurately
* Reduce human errors in financial records
* Improve inventory monitoring
* Speed up the checkout process
* Provide quick access to customer history and balances
* Generate meaningful reports for business owners

---

## ✨ Features

### 👥 Customer Management

* Create and manage customer profiles
* Store contact information
* View customer transaction history
* Track outstanding balances

### 💳 Credit Sales Management

* Register credit sales
* Track unpaid invoices
* Record customer payments
* Calculate remaining balances automatically

### 📦 Product & Inventory Management

* Product catalog management
* Inventory tracking
* Stock updates after sales
* Barcode support

### 🧾 Sales Management

* Cash sales
* Credit sales
* Sales history
* Detailed transaction records

### 📊 Reporting & Analytics

* Sales reports
* Debt reports
* Customer balance summaries
* Inventory reports

### 🔍 Search & Productivity

* Fast customer search
* Fast product search
* Barcode scanning
* Optimized workflows for daily store operations
